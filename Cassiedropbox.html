Artist Statement

Cassandra Graf is a graduate of Herron School of Art and Design, Indianapolis. She currently resides in Fort Wayne, Indiana, where she specializes in creating unique, customized portraits of pets for patrons. She also works to create powerful emotional connections through one of a kind original pieces.
